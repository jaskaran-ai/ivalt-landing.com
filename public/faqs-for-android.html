Android Registration Guide

Follow these steps to complete your iVALT registration on Android

Important Notice

You MUST ALLOW all Notifications during Registration.

1

Start Registration

Click "Start" to begin the registration process.

2

Enter User Information

Fill in your details and check "Terms and Conditions".

3

Proceed to Next Step

Click "Next" to continue with the registration.

4

Enter Verification Code

A 4-digit registration code will be sent to your phone. Enter this number and click "Submit".

Critical Step

YOU MUST GO TO YOUR EMAIL NOW to click on the verification link in the iVALT email before you can successfully click the "Next" button on your mobile phone.

6

Email Verification Complete

After clicking the email verification link, you'll get a confirmation message on your screen and receive an email confirmation.

7

Complete Registration

Click "Next" to complete your registration (button is now enabled).

8

Congratulations

You will receive a "Congratulations" page confirming successful registration.

9

Access Your Account

Click on "Go to Home Page" to start using the mobile app.

10

Troubleshooting

If you entered incorrect user information, uninstalling and re-installing the mobile app can help resolve the issue.
